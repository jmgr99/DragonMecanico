Home

Welcome to binyan.im, a place dedicated to the study of Hebrew language in general, and to the Hebrew verbs in particular. This website has the following sections:

Learn the binyanim

This section teach you the conjugation of the main seven binyanim (Qal, Nifal, Piel, Pual, Hifil, Hufal, Hitpael).

Practice

This section allows you to practise the conjugation in a dinamic way with two games
 

  • Analyse: A game where you have to analyse random verbs, and guess their person, number, mood and binyan.
    •  
  • Conjugate: A game where you have to conjugate random verb forms after being provided with their person, number, mood and binyan.
    • Note: for this game you will need a Hebrew keyboard and the niqqud. If you don't know how to use the later, check the section below: How to write the niqqud (vowels).
